WHO ARE WE AND WHAT IS OUR VISION?

We (Ruchira Saha and Astha Patel) are a team of two women from India, who want all our fellow women to be safe, independent, self-aware and self-reliant. Both of us are undergraduate students, pursuing Data Science and Programming.

India has 28 states and 8 union territories. Each state has its own set of emergency contact numbers. Many of us aren't aware of the emergency contact numbers of the individual states. This website was created with a view to educate everyone about the same.

The app "You are safe" was created by us to enable women to send their location to their trusted contact numbers when they feel unsafe. It has been observed in a case study funded by Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, that most of the cases of violence generally happen during the day in which the woman is out of the home, alone for livelihood activities, without no family members being around.This app will ensure that they receive assistance(if needed) at the earliest and thus, help to bring down the cases of crime against women
